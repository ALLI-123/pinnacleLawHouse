About Pinnacle Law House

Excellence in Legal Service Delivery Since Inception

PINNACLE LAW HOUSE is known for providing prompt, responsive and cost-effective legal services within Lagos, Abeokuta, Ibadan, Akure and the FCT. We also provide Cross Border Legal Representation; providing advisory, litigation, dispute resolution and other support services within the West African Corridor through partnership aimed at meeting up clients' demand.

Our Vision

We strive to be trusted problem solvers to our clients. We tell our clients what they need to hear, not what they want to hear. We pride ourselves on our responsive, honest and cost effective yet efficient services.

Our Mission

Based on our core values, we pride ourselves on delivery of top-notch legal service. Our Clients are treated with dignity and respect. We also give back to the community by donating time, money and legal expertise to those in dire need of it.

Why Retainership?

Wouldn't you have a retainership relationship with us? Doing that will save you the hurdles of searching for a firm of lawyers to deal with your legal related issues when you are in dire need of legal guidance. We are at your service. It is you first! We are just a call or message away from you.
